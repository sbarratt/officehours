Office Hours

About

We built a platform for students to find other students to study with.

Students can post their location, room number, a start and end time and class to the portal. Other students can see student-run office hours that other people are hosting and go and study with them.

It is an idea we have had for a while, and have wanted to be implemented and available for us to use.

Built at Big Red Hacks 2016.
